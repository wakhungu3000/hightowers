Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Is the first consultation free? +

Yes. We offer a complimentary introductory consultation so we can understand your needs and explain how we can help — with no pressure and no obligation.

How confidential is the information I share? +

Completely confidential. All client information is handled under strict confidentiality agreements. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.

Do you serve clients outside Nairobi? +

Absolutely. We work with clients across Kenya and are fully equipped to conduct consultations remotely via phone, Zoom, or WhatsApp — distance is no barrier to great financial advice.

What documents should I bring to a consultation? +

For a first consultation, you don't need anything specific — just come with your goals and questions. For deeper advisory work, we may request payslips, bank statements, or business financials.

How do your fees work? +

Our fees vary by service type and engagement scope. We are transparent about pricing from the outset — there are no hidden costs. Contact us for a customised quote based on your specific needs.
