Who We Are

Kenya's Trusted Financial Advisory Partner

Hightowers Financial Consultants is a trusted financial advisory firm dedicated to helping individuals and businesses achieve financial stability and long-term success.

We combine deep industry knowledge, practical experience, and a thorough understanding of Kenya's financial ecosystem to deliver solutions that are both effective and sustainable — not just in the short term, but for years to come.

Founded in Nairobi, we have grown from a boutique advisory practice into a firm that serves hundreds of clients across personal, business, and institutional segments, maintaining the same client-first philosophy from day one.
